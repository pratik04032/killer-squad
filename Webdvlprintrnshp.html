Web Developer Internship Opportunities at Cognifyz Technologies

Overview

Join Cognifyz Technologies for an exciting Web Developer Internship program designed to provide hands-on experience in a dynamic and innovative environment. This program spans 3 months, offering you the opportunity to work on real projects and develop your skills.

Internship Highlights

  • Gain real-world experience by working on live projects
  • Receive mentorship from experienced professionals
  • Collaborate with a talented and diverse team
  • Enhance your resume and professional network

Qualifications

We are looking for passionate and driven individuals with the following skills:

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Familiarity with front-end frameworks like React or Angular
  • Basic understanding of back-end technologies such as Node.js or Python
  • Strong problem-solving abilities and a willingness to learn
